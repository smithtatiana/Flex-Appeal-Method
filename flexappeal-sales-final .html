THE FLEXAPPEAL™ METHOD

Three Pillars.
One Hourglass.

The FlexAppeal™ system is built on three pillars — each targeting a specific part of the hourglass shape.

◈
PILLAR ONE
Expand the Frame

Build shoulder caps and lat width to create the illusion of a smaller waist. Width up top makes everything below look more sculpted and defined.

◉
PILLAR TWO
Refine the Center

Train the core for control, stability, and posture — without overdeveloping the waist. Strategic core work that sculpts without bulking.

▽
PILLAR THREE
Build the Base

Develop glutes and lower body to create the curves and balance that complete the hourglass. The foundation everything else is built on.
